My collection of model die-cast cars has roughly of 250 cars from different eras. On this site you can find a few of them. I divided my collection into 3 parts:

  • Vintage - 1900 - 1940
  • Classics - 1940 - 1990
  • Modern - 1990 - present
Most of the collection are cars from central and eastern Europe, but there are some examples from America and Asia as well.
